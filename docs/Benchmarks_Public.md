# CORE Benchmarks

CORE is evaluated against a set of benchmark suites designed to measure properties specific to its architectural goals.

Benchmark harness code, scoring functions, suite names, pass/fail thresholds, and methodology details are maintained in the private repository.

---

## Evaluation Goals

The benchmark suites collectively assess whether CORE achieves the properties it is designed for:

- Structural permanence of corrections
- Long-horizon memory reliability
- Representational integrity across operations
- Pipeline safety under normal and adversarial conditions
- Stability of architectural properties as scale increases

These are the properties that matter. The specific methodology for measuring them is proprietary.
